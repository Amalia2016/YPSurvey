Primary Objectives

  1. Study the relationship between features such as personality traits, phobias, demographics, preferences, health and spending habits.

  2. Implement statistical models to predict preferences, spending and health habits.

Secondary Objectives

  1. Show the process of analyzing survey data in a structured way.

  2. Perform a walkthrough of different statistical techniques.

Questions and Answers

  1. The analysis and modeling will try to answer questions such as: if some of user's traits or preferences are known, can we predict a behaviour (habit)?
