Overview of the Data

View of the data

Description

  • The data file consists of 1010 rows and 150 columns.

  • Columns have a short and a long name.

  • The long name explains the meaning.

  • 135 columns are ordinal (Values: 1 to 5).

  • 4 columns are numeric (integer). E.g: age.

  • 11 columns are categorical.

Features

  • Music preferences (19 items)

  • Movie preferences (12 items)

  • Hobbies & interests (32 items)

  • Phobias (10 items)

  • Health habits (3 items)

  • Personality traits, views & opinions (57 items)

  • Spending habits (7 items)

  • Demographics (10 items)

Other Information

  • The data contain missing values.

  • The original questionnaire was in Slovak language and was later translated into English.

  • All participants were of Slovakian nationality, aged between 15-30.
